# GlobalArtisansHub

Welcome to Global Artisans Hub, a platform dedicated to showcasing exquisite handmade and handicraft products created by talented women artisans from around the world. Our mission is to empower women, particularly from tribal communities, by providing them with a sustainable and fair-trade marketplace. Through our platform, we aim to preserve traditional crafts, promote economic independence, and celebrate the rich cultural heritage of our artisans.
